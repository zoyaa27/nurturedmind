Focus Corner

This is the Focus Corner. It is designed to help participants concentrate on daily activities. You can stay online with the Focus Corner while you complete homework, study, and engaging in many other activities. This program is designed not only to help students, but also anyone who needs help prioritizing staying productive. Add tasks you would like to do and check them off as you go!

Tip: The Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that divides work into focused intervals, typically 25 minutes long, separated by short breaks, usually 5 minutes. After four of these 25-minute work intervals (a "pomodoro"), a longer break of 15-30 minutes is taken. Use this method to improve focus, minimize distractions, and prevent burnout!
